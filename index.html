NOTES

You are Drew, the tax-paying half-japanese photographer.

The fire alarm is going off in your urban apartment building, so you are now standing outside (with your camera, as always). You are upset because the fire alarm goes off almost every day, everyone has to evacuate, yet to date, the firefighters have found no fires...

You see Brianna outside the apartment, who lives on the floor below you. You have a crush on Brianna, but she is unfortunately obsessed with the local superhero HAMHOCK, who notoriously crushes evil with his giant, beautiful, muscular thighs. Brianna is crying outside the apartment because she can't find her cat, Havana.

While she would prefer to stay and see if HAMHOCK shows up, Brianna agrees to go on a walk with you to look for Havana. With Brianna, you must explore, use your camera to zoom in on the details, find her cat, and figure out what's going on with this fire alarm situation before your camera runs out of battery...
